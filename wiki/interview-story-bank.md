# Interview story bank

Use these as raw material for interview answers. Keep answers concise: situation, task, action, result.

## Production debugging and rollout coordination

- Source: Lead Software Engineer at PT Inter Sistem Asia.
- Situation: NCCF/ERP data flows supported core order-management workflows during UAT, rollout, and change windows.
- Task: Protect production workflows while coordinating releases and debugging issues.
- Action: Led debugging, coordinated release decisions, aligned validation and rollout trade-offs across internal, client, ERP, and business stakeholders.
- Result: Core order-management workflows were protected during critical release periods.

Use for:

- "Tell me about a difficult production issue."
- "How do you handle pressure?"
- "How do you coordinate with stakeholders?"
- "How do you decide trade-offs?"
